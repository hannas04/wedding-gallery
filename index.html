Our Story

Three chapters, one grove

Chapter One

The first meeting

A rained-out train and a shared table at a café outside Lecce. One of them ordered the wrong coffee on purpose, just so the conversation would last a little longer. It worked.

Chapter Two

The first date

A picnic between the olive rows that turned into four hours, then a second bottle of wine, then a promise to do it again the following week. We never stopped.

"Ti ho riconosciuto prima ancora di conoscerti." — I recognized you before I even knew you.

From their proposal letter
Chapter Three

The proposal

Same olive grove, four years later, at the exact hour the light turns gold. Nosazena said yes before Oluwatomisin finished the question — and made them finish it anyway.

Travel & Stay

Getting here, and where to sleep

Getting there

Fly into Murtala Muhammed Intl (LOS), about 45 minutes from the venue.

We recommend booking a trusted taxi or shuttle — Lagos traffic can be busy, and a private transfer will be available for guests on Saturday.

Where to stay

Eko Hotel & Suites — 25 min drive, our room block closes 1 August.

Radisson Blu Lagos — boutique option near the waterfront, 30 min drive.

Local tips

September in Lagos runs warm — pack light layers and comfortable shoes for city streets.

Leave a morning free for Lekki Market or the waterfront; both are worth the wander.
