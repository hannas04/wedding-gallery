Chapter One

Our Story

01

How We Met

A mutual friend's birthday dinner, a table too small for the group, and a seat that happened to open up next to hers. Oluwatomisin still insists it was fate; Nosazena still insists it was the seating chart.

02

The First Date

Suya and slow conversation that ran two hours past when either of them meant to leave. They talked about everything and nothing, and both went home already looking forward to the next one.

03

The Proposal

On a rooftop at golden hour, with their closest people hiding just out of sight, Nosazena got down on one knee. Oluwatomisin said yes before the question was even finished.

"I didn't know a person could feel like a home until I met you."

— from Nosazena's letter to Oluwatomisin
